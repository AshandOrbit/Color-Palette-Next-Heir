Brand Architecture

  • Gold — Element United brand constant. Trust, premium, unifying thread across all products.
  • Burgundy — NextHeir core. Legacy, warmth, heritage. Used on most NextHeir pages.
  • Rose/Blush — Weddings vertical. Softer, romantic, feminine.
  • Emerald — STRIA Registry. Verification, sustainability, trust infrastructure.
  • Leather — Custom vault personalization. Warm, rustic, individual family character.
